# Changelog

## ReforgedArmory-TBC 1.15-tbc - 2026-07-19

- Added compatibility with WoW Classic Anniversary TBC and ElvUI 15.18.
- Restored the WrathArmory-style expanded character layout and statistics panel.
- Added item levels to every equipped item.
- Restored gem, enchant, GearScore, average item-level, durability, and resistance displays.
- Restored the character-title selector.
- Added safe compatibility handling for TBC character-frame APIs that do not expose retail model-scene or border objects.
- Guarded hooks for APIs that do not exist on the TBC client.
- Enabled and integrated the bundled GearScore library.
- Matched TacoTip's GearScore coefficients, rarity thresholds, slot weighting, hunter adjustments, and Titan's Grip handling.
- Added GearScore and average item-level display for inspected players.
- Disabled the belt-buckle warning in TBC, where belt buckles do not exist.
- Updated the addon identity to ReforgedArmory-TBC while retaining the original internal module key for settings compatibility.
